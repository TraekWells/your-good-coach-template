What is Imposter Syndrome?

Imposter syndrome is the feeling of self-doubt and inadequacy despite evident success. Many people, even highly accomplished individuals, experience this psychological pattern.

Common Signs

  • Feeling like a fraud despite achievements
  • Attributing success to luck rather than skill
  • Fear of being exposed as an "imposter"

How to Overcome It

Here are some strategies to combat imposter syndrome:

  1. Recognize Your Thoughts: Identify negative self-talk and challenge it.
  2. Celebrate Your Successes: Keep a record of accomplishments.
  3. Talk About It: Share your feelings with trusted friends or mentors.
